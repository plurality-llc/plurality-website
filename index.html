About

Plurality LLC is a solo software studio and holding company. The focus is on building products that serve specific gaps in fast-moving markets, where being small and fast is actually an advantage over bigger, slower teams.

The studio covers the full stack, from product strategy and engineering to API design, growth, and distribution.
